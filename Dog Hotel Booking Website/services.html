What We Offer

Premium Services for Your Dog

Every service is designed to keep your dog happy, healthy, and absolutely adored.

🚶

Daily Dog Walking

Structured daily walks in our private nature trails led by certified handlers. Exercise, exploration, and fresh air every single day.

🛁

Grooming & Spa

Full grooming sessions including bathing with premium shampoos, blow-dry, nail trimming, and coat styling by professional groomers.

📡

24/7 Pet Monitoring

HD live cameras in all suites. Track your dog's activities in real time through our mobile app with daily photo & video reports.

🌿

Outdoor Play Area

Spacious, securely fenced outdoor areas with agility equipment, splash pools, and climbing structures for active dogs.

🚐

Pick-up & Drop-off

Convenient door-to-door luxury pet transportation in climate-controlled vehicles. Available within 30 km from our location.

🏥

24/7 Veterinary Care

Fully-equipped on-site veterinary clinic staffed around the clock. Routine health checks and emergency care always available.
